Section 1:

Accordance to Law

All rules and laws stated shall be applicable to everyone in accordance to being in print below. Everyone (or when applied to specific parties) must obey these laws set forth and violation of set laws, rules, or agreements shall result in punishment by the Republic Legislation. These laws are not null and void and shall not be changed unless authorized by and only by leader personnel.

All members shall and must agree to the set rules and agreements, by stating (“I agree.”) verbally on recording or on text stated as listed in the authorized agreement box, personnel will accept all terms and conditions that are applied and set forth. No personnel shall disagree nor shall say otherwise, if personnel do not agree to set rules, they shall be subject to including but not limited to, punishments, verbal warnings, and termination of any certifications or statuses. Permission to punish is granted to and only to leader personnel who shall make such decisions.


Section 2:

General Disciplinary Agreement (G.R.A)

Article 1:

By being a personnel of the 104th, you agree to but not limited to, verbal punishment, derankment, PT, and termination of statuses, badges, and or certifications. You must proceed with pending punishments, refusal to do so will result in harsher punishment or permanent termination of personnel status.

Article 2:

Disciplinary officers, if granted permission by leader personnel, shall execute punishments and shall verbally warn any parties or personnel who break listed laws and agreements.

Article 3:

In the case the 104th initiates an on duty event, training event, or war all personnel and parties must respect and act in accordance to the (G.R.A) standards.

HICOM Certificate Agreement (H.C.A)

Article 1:

HICOMs’ in accordance with the (H.C.A) must obey all rules listed and meet stated certification requirements. HICOM is a status which means it shall be removed at any point in time, by and only by leadership personnel.

Article 2:

As part of personnel who are agreeing to become a certified HICOM, HICOMS shall not discuss private information from disclosed meetings, certified HICOMs are required to understand the written rules and laws, and to have it in sight when needed or requested.

Fairness Trial Agreement (F.T.A)

Article 1:

All personnel and parties of the 104th must be non-partial and have non-biased viewpoints while giving evidence or information. Personnel who are part R.L.A must respect and obey this rule, failure to do so will result in termination of R.L.A status or certification.


Section 3:

Party Classification System (P.C.S)

Article 1:

All personnel must follow the P.C.S system, refusal or failure to do so will result in but not limited to, verbal punishment, derankment, PT and termination of statuses, badges, and or certifications. Parties shall not merge or split apart. Personnel in parties shall not use their parties to revolt, if such a revolution happens, all members will be deranked heavily, become an untrusted member, and or get permanently exiled from the group, the leader or personnel that started the revolution will be executed by firing squad.

Article 2:

All personnel in accordance with their party must follow their written restrictions and privileges. Failure or refusal to do so will result in but not limited to, verbal punishment, derankment, PT and termination of statuses, badges, and or certifications.

Leader Authority Rule (L.A.R)

Article 1:

Leadership shall not be gained nor removed nor shall anyone grant leadership responsibilities to any other party(ies) and to any and all personnel or outsiders, leadership is not a status, badge, or certification, but a permanent authority mark on disclosed personnel, (i.e. mellowfellow17 and Spawn) only.

Article 2:

Leaders are never denied nor temporarily respected, all parties must respect and treat the leaders with the highest level of respect, (for hereinafter (“respect”) means to treat the leader personnel with dignity and to act like a mature and professional soldier). Breaking such rule will result in but limited to PT, deranking, verbal warnings, and termination of statuses, badges, and or certifications. Leader personnel and only leader personnel are allowed to execute listed punishments. Unless leader personnel grants permission to other parties to go through with punishment.

Article 3:

All ranks must obey leader personnel and follow all orders given by leader personnel. Refusal to do so will result in but not limited to PT, deranking, verbal warnings, and termination of statuses, badges, and or certifications. No other party, personnel, and or outsiders shall tell people what to do, with the exception of disciplinary officers.

Republic Legislative Authority (R.L.A)

Article 1:

Personnel who have gotten HICOM certified and are granted permission by leader personnel shall work and be part of the Republic legislative system.

Article 2:

Personnel who created biased rulings or do a subpar job will be subject to termination of certification or status. This shall only be granted once leader personnel have agreed to allow the status, the member has met all requirements, and took the certification test and passed it.

Republic Guard Force (R.G.F)

In the case there is a revolution, a rogue member, or civilians raid or attack the 104th, lethal force will have to be enacted. R.G.Fs purpose is to protect the 104th and to prevent personnel from becoming rogue, these guards shall be stationed and enforce the laws set forth. If one shall disrespect or attack an RG, the R.G.F shall have full authority to neutralize the threat, unless prohibited or denied by leader personnel. This shall only be granted once leader personnel have agreed to allow the status, the member has met all requirements, and took the certification test and passed it.

Republic disciplinary Force (R.D.F)

The R.D.F shall be set to maintain the needed respect and professionalism, members of the R.D.F shall conduct PT that was requested by leader personnel or suggested by an R.D.F and approved by leader personnel. This shall only be granted once leader personnel have agreed to allow the status, the member has met all requirements, and have taken the certification test and passed it.
